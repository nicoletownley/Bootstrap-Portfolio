About Me

As a Native of Minnesota, I have come to love nature. I will travel far and wide to experience all of nature's diversity. Similarily, I have an appreciation for the diversity of intellect and culture. These I also seek at all ends of the earth. So, I am well traveled, at least in this hemisphere. I intend to travel for business and with my friends and family. Visiting Asia, Europe and Africa will teach me to become a renaissance woman; in our global economy, but most importantly, in life.
